The Structural Case

Why Brazil wins the nearshore argument

Four factors that together cannot be replicated by any other nearshore region at this price point.

UTC-3: full US and EU overlap

Brazil operates at UTC-3 year-round, with real-time synchronous overlap across both continents:

  • US East Coast (ET): UTC-3 = ET+2 — covers 9 AM to 6 PM ET with zero async lag.
  • US West Coast (PT): UTC-3 = PT+5 — 6 AM–2 PM PT synchronous window; afternoon async, context written.
  • Western Europe (CET/CEST): UTC-3 = CET−4 — 9 AM CET standup is 6 AM São Paulo; full overlap to 6 PM CET.
  • UK (GMT/BST): UTC-3 = GMT−3 — 10 AM GMT standup is 7 AM São Paulo; synchronous to 5 PM GMT.

No 24-hour ticket cycles. No async-only standups. Live decisions, live unblocking — whether your team is in New York, San Francisco, London or Frankfurt.
